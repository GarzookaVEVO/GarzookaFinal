Chamber of Reflection

One of the modern champions of indie rock and "soft rock", Mac DeMarco is one of the worlds most famous "slackers", even if he really doesn't slack by going on tours and writing albums, his persona of being one is as popular as his music. His warbly often love packed music has wormed its way into every corner of the internet, I'm sure you've heard a slowed down version of 'Chamber of Reflection' over some meme on TikTok at least once.

Here Comes The Demo Cowboy

One of my favorite parts of Mac DeMarco's catalogue is the presence of demo tapes for each album he produces is a really interesting look into what the song could have been, as well as holding the low quality charm of a demo tape, the everpresent hiss and all.
